Photography Services & Projects

Hi! I'm Alfie, a 21-year-old Student (University of Warwick, MMathPhys) and Professional Photographer. I specialise in Event and Studio work, and pride myself on my friendly and charismatic approach to projects. Let me know if I can be of service! 😊

It's a pleasure to meet you!

I still remember the day I got my first camera as a kid, and how excited I was to take it for a spin. I spent hours that day running through the local woods and taking terrible photos of anything and everything, grinning from ear to ear at the over-exposed blur I'd just snapped.

I've come a long way since that day (as has my gear!) but that child-like fascination is unwavering. I put that passion into every photo I take, yearning to bring the best out of any situation I'm in, and spreading that excitement along the way - making physical memories that stand the test of time.

What to Expect at an Event

Presence

I am a confident and directive photographer who brings passion and focus to every event. I position myself strategically throughout the venue, capturing authetic candids and anticipating key moments before they happen. While never intrusive, I'm hard pressed to have a memorable moment go by uncaptured, ensuring your event has all the highlights you remember.

Charisma

My primary aim at an event is to become a well-integrated, friendly guest, whom attendees feel comfortable around. Between snaps, I'm constantly building rapport, scouting the venue for that perfect location, and brainstorming ways to bring out the best in each person and moment.

Attention to Detail

I strive to make each photo I take personal and compelling, often communicating with guests directly to ensure I'm capturing their authentic selves. After your event, I'll conscientiously select and edit roughly 100-150 photos in my signature style (providing necessary touch-ups along the way) and aim to deliver the final collection in under a week.
